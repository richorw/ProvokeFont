PROVOKE

A Display Typeface Hommage to Provoke Magazine

'Purovōku'

Provoke font originates from the logotype of Provoke Magazine, a Japanese photography magazine in 1970s, which spreads a completely new idea of photography in Japan.

Provocative Materials for Thought

Multi-language Support

Besides basic A to Z, Provoke provides various diacritics for multiple Western languages.
